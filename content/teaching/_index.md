## ETH Zurich

### Graduate Courses

**Research Design and Qualitative Methods**
*Department of Humanities, Social and Political Sciences*

This course covers the logic of qualitative inquiry, case selection, process tracing, qualitative comparative analysis (QCA), and mixed-methods research design. Students learn to formulate testable hypotheses, evaluate evidence systematically using Bayesian reasoning, and design research that produces credible causal inferences from small-N studies.

### Undergraduate Courses

**Contemporary European Politics**
*Department of Humanities, Social and Political Sciences*

An introduction to the institutions, politics, and policy challenges of the European Union and its member states. Topics include democratic backsliding, the politics of enlargement, the EU's response to external crises, and the future of European integration.

## University of Lucerne

**European Security amid Great Power Competition**
*University Lecturer*

This course examines how competition among the United States, China, and Russia is reshaping the European security order. It covers NATO adaptation, EU defense initiatives, energy security, and the institutional architecture of European security governance.
